Contractor Plan

Contains Fire prevention and protection measure, actions for fire appendix 1, assembly point with signage.

Housekeeping: Smoking and Flames

Rubbish and waste kept min, comply with STD-206 housekeeping , no smoking within 25m of refueling area, no engine running when refueling, safe refueling practices, no phone permitted during refueling.

Electricity

Install by competent, isolated after working hour, defective put out of use, wiring leads plugs good condition, distribution panels secured and marked, home-made extension lead forbidden, compatible plus with sockets permitted, lighting cover.

Gas Cylinders

Cylinder values closed when work finished, empty, moved, gauges removed and valve caps when moving cylinder, cages for lifting cylinders, not placed to be a part of electrical circuit, not taken to confined space, store gas in safe manner, cylinders stored in ventilated and shaded 25m away flammable stores, LPG away 6.1m form gas cylinder, protected from falling or high temperature, no smoking within 25m, standard227 for welding

Tire

not store in place where hot works is conducted..

Flammable liquids

combustible stored original container with labeled contents and capacity, metal or non-static producing containers, not used near ignition sources, approved containers, permanent fuel storage maintained, fuel lines has valves capable of stopping the flow and withstand pressure, tanks protected against collision, flammable liquid transfer approved pump

Offices

5m between building, smoke detector fire extinguishers, no smoking in office, access and exit are clear from any materials, exits are signed

Stores

Grounded/earthed, areas clean, obstruct access to fire protection, aisles/hallways 91cm wide, material 1.8m from doorway, 202 standards, materials storing per MSDS, Vehicle /Sheds/Garages/Workshops: complied with all vehicle, standard201

Means of Escape

There secondary fire exits, escape is clear from obstruction, escape routes, locked with a key, lit with natural light or domestic.

Training of Personnel

Aware of location of assembly point and firefighting locations, Toolbox talk training all personnel, contractor’s personnel are competent using firefighting, supervisor trained in emergency procedures.

Fire Alarm / Detection Systems

Effective warnings of fire (shouting, bell, break glass alarm, visual alarm, automatic fire detection must be used in offices.

Firefighting Equipments

(Relevant equipment located: 1 per building, every floor, 1 within 15.1m flammable liquid, 15.1m flammable gas, 1 every 22.9m open storage, ), storage have portable fire extinguishers, fire point identified, only trained personnel use fight fires, replace extinguishers replaced after discharged, use right type for the fire class.

Water supply

contractor know location of fire water.

Action taking in fire

personal discovering fire (raise alarm, attack fire if trained and safe, trained personnel assist) All other staff: evacuated move to assembly point, supervisor ensure no person left inside, ensure all employees are there, project manager instruct personnel to stay, By Security: fire alarm sounding, emergency services, allow no access, !

Fire Classes
