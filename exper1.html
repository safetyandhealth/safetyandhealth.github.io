Environment, Safety & Health Training Rotation at Bechtel SABCO

Site Visit


The safety lessons learnt and safety procedures performed during this site visit are the following:

1- Check the quarter safety audit updates of the contractor including but not limited to:
A- Traffic Management Plan.
B- Emergency Breakdown Plan.
C- Plan for inspecting vehicle loads.
D- Safety Education, training and course developed for Employees.

2- Check the Clinic, nurse, medical equipment, safety conditions in clinic, medications supply, welfare in clinic and inspect for any safety hazards in the clinic to ensure RC standards are met.
3- Check the housekeep of the contractor site, signs, welfare, smoking shelter.
4- Check the storage, the chemicals and material stored inside. Notify the contractors of any concerns to ensure proper storage is in place. Instruct the contractor to avoid overstocking of material above the allowed safe limit, raise issue regarding proper housekeeping and proper storage of material.

5- Check the routes around the contractor site and raise the issue of separating the pedestrians walk site by using pedestrian barricades.
6- Check and notify employees about the importance of wearing their PPE during their existence in the worksite.

7- Check the ambulance, its log, and instruct the contractor to have the ambulance to be daily check.
8- Ask for update about the required safety officer in the site with rate of 1 officer for each 50 employees.
9- Check the safety of driving the site work and inform the safety officer to provided required training for employees.

10- Check the excavation in the site and ensure the it is met the required safety standard to have barricades and warning signs around it.
11- Ensure the area with waste are barricaded and with warning sign.
12- Check that the roads have the required speed signs, and warning signage i.e. detour to ensure the drivers be warned.
13- Check a Man-lift equipment operator, check his license, check that if the equipment is operated in accordance to RC standards and check the vehicle inspection.
