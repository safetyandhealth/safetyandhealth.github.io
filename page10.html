Environment, Safety & Health Training Rotation at Bechtel SABCO


Man-flits

(Mobile Elevating Work Platforms (MEWPs) and Articulating Boom Platforms), scissor lift, self-propelled boom, vehicle mounted boom, trailer mounted boom, vertical personnel platform and insulated aerial device.

Training

Operator competent and trained examination/certification, other responsible personnel on site trained to use the man-lift ground control to lower the platform in emergency situation.

Safe Working

Man-lifts shall be inspected by 3ed party on annual basis
Display a sticker corresponding to 3ed party inspection certificate
Manufacturer's instruction manual must be on site for each and every man-lift in use Inspection performed and documented on daily basis
Man-lift used for lifting personnel and small tools in designed safe working load
platform not overload only safe working load, wear full-body harness
personnel must stay inside the working platform at all times
Use of staging or ladders to extend height is strictly forbidden
Must equipped with a 5-kg fire extinguisher
Safety devices removed or disabled (test limit switches, alarms, flashing beacons)

Specific Man-Lifts are fitted
Work area surrounding the man-lift is to be cordoned off using barricades and signage
Strictly forbidden to move any Man-lift within the following distances of overhead power lines unless de-energized
Documentary evidence for SECO for de-energized the overhead power line
All man-Lifts are designed to operate in wind speeds up to a maximum which must be marked on the equipment
Type of Man-lifts may be traveled with the platform raised for greater operator visibility
Personnel will not tie the platform off to any structure for any reason
Operator should be in the basket at all time during operation
