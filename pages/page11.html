Environment, Safety & Health Training Rotation at Bechtel SABCO


Definition

Access & Egress

Include ascent and descent.

Supporting Structure

Structure, temporary or permanent, used for the purpose of supporting working platform, including construction plant and scaffolding.

Working Platform

Any platform used as a place as access or egress including any scaffold, suspended scaffold, cradle, mobile platform, man-basket, trestle, gangway, gantry and stairway.

Standards

Any surface upon supporting structure shall be stable and sufficient composition to safety support structure/load/platform, shall be of sufficient strength and rigidity for the purpose it is being used, all working platform or decking shall be closely boarded to their full width and free from tripping hazards, when material is deposited on platform, a clear passage of 440mm must be maintained, the space between platform and structure no more than 300mm, guard rails must be provided for all platform, platforms with scaffold comply with standard 215, platform should not have more than 20mm gap between boards, guard rails height between 960-1140 mm, ladder with platform must be 1 m above the working platform, erected by competent persons, platform opening must be protected with an access hatch.
