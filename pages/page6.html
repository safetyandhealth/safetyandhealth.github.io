Contraction Plant

All operated mechanical powered plant (dozers, tractors, graders, excavators, dumpers, cranes, articulating)

Vehicles

light, medium & heavy goods rigid/articulated buses personnel pick=ups cars water vessels boats and barges

Green-man

to keep safe distance from equipment and vehicle .

Red-man

permitted work-zone of equipment/vehicle .

General Guidance

Contractor submit Traffic Management Plan (TMP) to RC be operating any vehicle , in TMP avoid routes from hazard/excavations/chemical storage, Contractor Management conduct ES&H weekly, JHA and risk assessments conducted, JHA addresses, when employees come to project conduct risk assessment, engine off when refueling, prevent mud dragged to public/private areas, all vehicle /equip have daytime running lights, establish rule to never pass stopped bus or multi passenger vehicle , set vehicle on parking engine off key remove, check equip before use in project.

Operator qualifications

Drivers of heavy must have driver license for heavy goods vehicle , crane operator valid Saudi in-date trained for specific crane, operator of heavy license for specific equipment they operate. All drivers have license.

Vehicle

Registered, licensed, maintained, operate in safe manner, seat-belt, lights(head, tail, turning, hazard warning) rearview mirrors, all tires visible tread, audible reversing alarm, spare tire, emergency repair tools, fire extinguisher, beacon and reversing alarm, no oil leakage, trailers must be secured, revers parked, no exceed max overload.

Construction Plant

Purpose and maintained in a safe manner, concrete pump machines to be 3ed party certified operator, provided safety equipment fitted, consideration given for the location and ground conditions, exhaust fumes vented safely, fuel tanks positioned in a safe area, grounding/earthed of electrical equipment.

Operation Procedures

Specific driving and plant operation rules to be developed by contractor, TMP provided by contractor within Construction phase ES&H, seat-belt in bus worn at all times, no eating/phoning/read/drinking during driving, speed limit, no overtake on site, headlights used all time, key to be removed from ignition.

Worksite Layout

Address by contractor during mobilization( barriers pedestrian and vehicle, safe crossing for pedestrian, speed limit, warning signs, no blind spot, min reversing operation, avoid of excavations, parking reversed, laydown yard), pedestrian areas only/routes accommodate employees, parking away for work location, welfare areas, flagman provided with sufficient lighting/ suitable shelter.

Training

Toolbox talks include defensive driving techniques, lessons learned conducted with drivers/operators to inform about incidents previous and RC reported, training records.

Loading/Unloading

Plan to minimize deliveries and double handling for material

Rules for Driving

I will not drive under influence of drugs/alcohol.
I will always wear my seatbelt and ensure also my passengers while driving.
I will NOT use mobile phone while driving
I will follow site traffic rules and speed limit (20 KPH)
I will follow posted speed limit signs on the public roadways
I will inspect my equipment/vehicle and document it before use, report any damage found.
I will wear my PPE when I leave the cab of my Equipment/Vehicle
I will never operate/drive any equipment or vehicle if I don’t have certificates, license or authorization
I will not leave my vehicle running unattended and will park it in a designated area
I will stop if people near to my equipment/vehicle
I will follow safe distance (10meter for haulage truck) or safe procedure while tipping.
I will lower my dump truck bed before I move
I will turn off my vehicle/equipment while refueling
I will report all accident/incident
I will not put any sunshade on my windshield that cause distraction on my visibility

Accident Reporting

Reported to Saudi Government, then To RC Security department 0133147777 within 20 minutes
