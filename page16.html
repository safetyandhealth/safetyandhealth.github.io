Environment, Safety & Health Training Rotation at Bechtel SABCO


Crane
Inspected by a 3ed party on annual basis, display a sticker of 3ed party inspection certificate, cranes have configuration changed shall be 3ed party tested before lifting can commence, every crane shall be fitted with an appropriate load radius indicator, automatic safe load indicators must be tested and examined after erection/installation of crane before use, fitted with hazard flashing beacons, shall be fitted with anti-two block device, crane shall cease operation when wind speed exceeds the safe operation level(above 32km/h then the load and boom lengths shall be reduced according to manufactures specs.), conduct daily pre-use inspections of their equipment by operator, crane manufacturer's manual will be kept onboard, ground conditions will be tested and verified to comply with crane manufacturers safe specs, all cranes/lifting equipment shall be checked at the start of the project or before using first time on the project.
Ground Conditions
When planning a lift consider: ( outrigger and track loading should be established before positioning of the crane, when positioning a crane in live operational areas and on concrete ground the safe load limit shall be identified against the specs of concrete foundations, outrigger pad sizes shall be applied as per crane manufacturers).
Lifting Operation Equipment
All lift operation equipment incepted by 3ed party yearly, inspected on a min 3-monthly basis by competent rigger/slinger and shall be maintained/identifying serial number/date of last inspection and name of inspector, conform with color codes (red, blue, yellow, green), inspected before use, qualified rigger/slinger are to rig the load to be lifted, lifting operation equipment, defective equipment shall be removed from site immediately for disposal.
Lifting operation
Standard 221 requirement shall be met during lifting operation, ensure relevant encroachment permits approved and available, during lifting mobile cell phones and pagers by anyone considered essential to the operation shall not be permitted, account wind conditions and rated load and boom lengths according to manufacture specs, prior to any lift a test lift shall be conducted(0.5m h and for 2 min), tag lines are to be used on all lifts, crane operator, all windows are to be free from curtains or obstruction to ensure that the operator has clear vision at all times, no person is permitted to be on or touching any load that is suspended.
Specific to Mobile Cranes
Outriggers used as specified by crane manufacture and fully extended on all sides when performing static lifting operation, sound timber packing or metal plates shall be positioned under each outrigger pad to distribute the load, edge of crane outrigger pads must be min 2m from edges of excavation or other bearing surfaces, safety clear zone consisting of the full length of jib + 10m should be identified 360 degrees around all cranes, fly jibs on telescopic cranes shall be erected or dismantled by a competent crane operator, anti-two block to be fitted and functioning, unless in an emergency situation/no lifting take place on roads and highways unless coordinated and approved by RC road Department, strictly forbidden to move any crane boom or load line within the following distances overhead power lines unless the line has been de-energized (up to 25000v 10m, over 25000 20m), Encroachment permit issued and approved by SECO, any lifting operation that within 15m of any water lines require an encroachment permit issued and approved by Marafiq.
Excavators
Will only be approved for lifting operation when(supplied with manufacturer's lifting point, supplied with a safe load indicator, has the safe working load, has a 3ed party inspection certificate, a task specific risk assessment).
Loader Crane (Hiab)
Used for loading and unloading of materials and equipment to and from their own payload area, hiab used on public roads for lifting operations shall comply with SA ministry , not for lifting people, anti-two block to be fitted and functioning.
Specific to friction cranes
Operator trained specific to that particular friction crane, checklist shall be completed for every lift make with a friction crane, operator and ground support personnel shall ensure that all personnel are removed from the swing path and that no personnel walk or work under a suspended load, 10 foot min distance from suspended load and great if the load height is more than 15 feet, friction team shall establish radio communications, when not being used for a lift/ the headache ball and main block if not tied back shall be lowered to within 8 feet from ground surface and 10 foot.
Specific to tower cranes
RC approval before install/erect/ use any tower crane for construction contracts, after approval submit the foundation design, prior to concrete pour of the foundation for the crane get RE to issue and sign compliance certificate app2, after curing the foundation RE issue and sign tower crane pre-erection inspection certificate app3, one the tower crane constructed and inspected by 3ed party before work may commence, all tower cranes have a lift plan containing separate lift calculations, aviation warning lights fitted, lift plans are to contain an emergency procedure for extraction an injured tower crane operator
