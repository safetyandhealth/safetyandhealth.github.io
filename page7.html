STD-209 Small Tools


General

Employees using hand, air, electrical tools shall be trained be competent, tools fit for purpose maintained/repaired/tested accordance with manufacturer instructions/ color inspection should be used, no hand-made or site fabricated tools are acceptable, face shield and hearing protection worn as required by risk assessment, tool must be inspected by user for defects, defective tools are taken out of service immediately, tools are not to be altered in any way, manufacturer supplied and fitted safety devices.

Operation Controls and Switches

Hand-held power tolls equipped with a constant-pressure or control that shuts off the power: drills/tappers, hand-held power tools such as circular saw holding accessories and constant-pressure switch.

Fuel powered or drivens

Gasoline powered tools not used in unventilated area, hearing/eye protection/safety boots , operator exposure to motorized vibrating equipment limited as per manufacturer, rotating parts to manufacture supplied guards, drain fuel when putting in storage or repair.

Electrical

Power tools must be operated in standing position not setting directly on ground, 110V or battery operated tools are to be used on construction site, Eye protection must be worn, Elec powered tools must be double insulated, temporary 110V tools shall be protected by ground, grinders shall be provided with hood-type guards with side enclosure/ the rated speed of grinding wheel check prior to use equal or greater than the maximum speed of the grinder, operator is prohibited from wearing loose clothing, tools given a unique site tool number and inspected quarterly.

Compressed Air

  • Pressure of compressed are for cleaning purposes reduced to 30 psi or less, compressed air must not be used for cleaning or blowing dust from any part of the body or pointed at a person, must equipped with a constant-pressure switch or control that shuts off the airflow when released by the user, compressed air hoses no connection apart from quick-release couplings, air supplying hoses exceeding 1-2 inch protected by excess flow valves, protected from damage, hearing protection worn.

Powder actuated

  • Pressure of compressed are for cleaning purposes reduced to 30 psi or less, compressed air must not be used for cleaning or blowing dust from any part of the body or pointed at a person, must equipped with a constant-pressure switch or control that shuts off the airflow when released by the user, compressed air hoses no connection apart from quick-release couplings, air supplying hoses exceeding 1-2 inch protected by excess flow valves, protected from damage, hearing protection worn.
