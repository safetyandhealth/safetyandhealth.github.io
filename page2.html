Environment, Safety & Health Training Rotation at Bechtel SABCO


General Guidance

  • Full-time dedicated competent person in charge
  • Environment protection considered on initial setting-up
  • Workshop machines installed tested
  • Commissioned maintained by competent person
  • Calibrated equipment and devices
  • Training for users
  • Inspect for defects before usage
  • Repair performed by competent persons,
  • Waste removed daily
  • Only-one day supply stored in workshop
  • Supply of PPE
  • Adequate ventilation and lighting
  • Onlywork related to contract construction activities are permitted in workshop
  • Sufficient quantities for firefighting

Engineering workshop: Woodworking and Metalworking Machine

Isolation electrical supply, isolator switch lockable and close to machine, experienced competent work on locked & tagged equipment, de-energized equipment when opened, emergency stop button easy reach (separated from start and stop buttons), fit for purpose and manufactured and operated per manufacturer instructions, floor around kept in good conditions, switch off when not in used, adequate space around machine to accommodate work-pieces waste items, lighting, access and escape routes, waste kept in scrap bins and emptied, wood dust is harmful and flammable clean the machine by operator after use, relevant PPE worn, machine with risk of eye injury guards in place during operation, risk of entanglement operated with a safety cover and no loose clothing when operating

Vehicle Workshops

Manager responsible for servicing and maintenance, serviceable equipment or vehicle . Not stored in workshops, drain equipment and vehicle of fuel before repair and oil fuel stored in correct containers, no person in vehicle inspection pit or under inspection ramp while positioning vehicle , bank-man or Redman to guiding the positioning of vehicle , cover inspection pit with sturdy covers, traffic management plan placed and displayed, logout and tag out system for hydraulic inspection.

Spray-Painting & Sand-Blasting

Dedicated area and consider vicinity and proximity during planning, electrical supplies and equipment flameproof, area screened off with non- flammable material and warning notice 25m away, no (smoking, eating, drinking) within 25m, 1-day supply paint thinners can be store , sand-blasting non-silica, all materials are under MSDS, first Aid at hand, PPE and RPE worn inspected used no-one is allowed without them (hearing, respiratory, face, eyes, skin), hearing protection, signage waring, oxygen supply and air filtration system checked inspected, compressors hoses and tolls comply with STD-208.
