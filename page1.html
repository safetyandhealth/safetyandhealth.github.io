Environment, Safety & Health Training Rotation at Bechtel SABCO


Safety standard in the construction site include:

Fenced
Secure access
Project Sign Board
Safety Alert & Posters, Site Signs, Contact numbers
Labor camp under RC Public Health Code
Laydown area plan required RC approval.

Vehicles in construction site

Speed 20 kph sign, speed limit, pedestrians and vehicles segregation, shaded car parking

Welfare

  • Rest areas
  • Smoking 25 m away from building
  • Chilled water dispensing area
  • Water tank for toilets
  • No food preparation
  • Signs Arabic and Englis
  • Cleaning and cooling of water coolers
  • Ice machines or freezers

Fuel Storage

10m away from watercourses, 50 from wells, tanks with sufficient strength and structural integrity, 750mm between bund wall and tank, 600mm between tank and base, un-reinforced materials is not recommended for bund wall construction, bulk fuels over 1drum 25m away building

Generator

Earthed, silent enclosed type, hear protection, signs, area secured only authorized access, exhaust pipe fumes into suitable area, maintenance records kept, 85dB 2m away, 25m away from building.

Gas Storage

Cylinders upright position, Sufficient sun protection, According to MSDS, Oxygen & acetylene 6m apart, LPG 6.1m away from other cylinders and 25m away, Gas storage area must not have other products like oil, Paints, Greases, Chemicals.

Chemical Storage

Correct signage, Accordance to MSDS, Adequate ventilation to allow fumes disperse naturally, Flammable/combustible 25m away building, No smoke 25m, Eye wash station.

Fire Prevention & Protection

5m spacing between building and structure, Smoke detectors install in offices, Fire extinguishers maintained and away from sunlight, Evaluation assembly

Security

Contractor comply with RC security and safety Dep. Rules and regulations, Site plan showing location of all hazardous area, Emergency procedures and phone numbers, Follow as-build drawings.
