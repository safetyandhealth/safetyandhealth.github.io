Environment, Safety & Health Training Rotation at Bechtel SABCO

Site Visit


The safety lessons learnt and safety procedures performed during this site visit are the following:

1- The egress and access to the excavation is more inclined steep.
2- The work location must be surrounded with hard barricade with warning signs.
3- Flagman must exist to guide the travel because the entrance of the work site is near road and there is crane for lifting.
4- A Duct Bank near and along the excavation is raising a serious issue because sand and soil are eroding beneath it.
Therefore, this is issue must be resolved soon.
5- Dewatering is performed where the water in the ground is pumped out from the excavation to ensure sure operations.

6- The safely working with crane is realized by calculating the crane capacity, load, angle.
7- Toilets and rest area must be close to construction site near workers and the rest area must be bigger.
8- Scaffolding for the valve chamber need a competent person to be erected.
9- Work above 1.8m, it required scaffolding.
10- Harness holds or hang on the basket in the man-lift above ground, while above the water, it should be taken off and the lifejacket must be wore.
11- Above water lifting is considered critical lifting.
