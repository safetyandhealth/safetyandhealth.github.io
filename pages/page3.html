Storage of Material

Prevent material sliding falling or collapsing, racking weight 250kg designed by a structural engineer, shelving shall not be overloaded, store in designated areas, store at 1.5m max, stack not higher than 3 times, min risk of manual handling injury, protected from the sun, waste placed in container immediately, load and unload from elevated location no one is wondering and barrier and sign, earthed/grounded, moving equip in storage manage equip and pedestrians, light protective covers, pack manageable weights

Housekeeping

Workplace Layout: in planning important space for materials, construction equipment and vehicle, no interference between redundant and new materials

Responsibilities of the Contractor

  • provide housekeeping control
  • eliminate waste
  • schedule time for housekeeping
  • monitor arrangement for house
  • correct poor housekeeping that has risk
  • ensure no risk for population and there is no health risk
  • inspect for defects before usage
  • repair performed by competent persons,
  • waste removed daily

General Guidance

offices and welfares cleaned, combustible and flammable waste removed, emergency exits and access paths clean, workplaces clean supervisor and foreman are responsible, storage of material under 202 standard, spillages removed immediately, control of electrical cables cords, extension, walkways and stairways clear, nails are removed, waste and scrap are segregated, hazardous waste collected appropriately and sign and barriers, prohibited to dump hazardous waste, cleaning rosters, burning of waste is forbidden.
