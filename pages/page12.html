Environment, Safety & Health Training Rotation at Bechtel SABCO


Detection of Buried Services

Services

Electrical, water, gas, telecommunications, sewage, surface water drainage, fiber optics etc.

Standards

Contractor conducts RA and Safe sequence of work for excavation including detection of buried services included in Construction Phase ES&H Plan

Obtain Utility Companies as-laid(Build) drawing showing where the services for verification, once Construction ES&H Plan given, site visit made to trace buried services, bear in mind that reference points may be moved/upgraded or not shown, using a cable detector, cable detector operators trained in the use, other indicators that buried services exist shall be used street lighting/illuminated traffic signs/manhole covers, cables or pipes may be laid loose in the ground, polythene tape or plastic mesh marked “danger – services buried below”,

Planned excavations marked out using point/tape/markers, pre-task briefing must be given by either the site supervisor or nominated competent person, A Utility Clearance Form (Permit – to-Dig) must be issued and signed by ES&H Manager/Office and Site Manager

Mechanical excavators and power tools must not be sued within 0.5m of the indicated line of the services/only hand digging is permitted, during excavation/closed/capped/sealed/loose services must always assume to be live or charged until proven otherwise, trench or running along a trench must be supported to prevent damage or stress, cable must never be used as anchorage points/footholds/climbing point, if a pipe or service is to move to continue the work the owner must be consulted beforehand, excavations and trenches must have suitable edge protection, as- laid(as-built) drawing provided by the utility company, contractor must update site drawing to show where services have been laid, any damage to buried services must be immediately reportedn
