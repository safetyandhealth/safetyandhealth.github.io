Method of Statement: Working at Night


Title: Method of Statement for Night Shift Work (Concrete Pouring for Tank Foundation).

General Description:

The scope of this MS is to cover the methodology of night shift work for Reinforced concrete pour activities.
Proper lighting, with tower lights as per plan that is agreed by RC Safety engineers, Truck movement controlled by Flagmen, Portable tower light placed around, Reinforced concrete pouring from 6:00 pm till next day early morning between 12-15 hours, 250- 300 trip of concrete mixer with 6 pumps, area of concrete wash out with tower lights and capacity of 8 trucks, efficient light for the Formwork installation area, Steel Rebar installation to be carried out with due care with high illumination.

Plant and Equipment:

Tower lights, Ambulance, Generator, Concrete pumps, Concrete Mixer, Hand Tools, Trovel, Wheel barrow.

Materials:

Dry Sand, Water, Concrete, Cement, Steel Rebars, Wood.

Site planning and Preparation:

Site management Plan ( Before start of work PM, CM, QM Superintendent, Foremen and SM, SO, discuss and plan the work in compliance to Safety and Quality, Site Inspection, All permit are available, after finalization of planning shall be discussed for further coordination.)

SM: responsible for (safety implementation, emergency response personnel is available).
Site/Field Engineer: supervise and monitor the works on site, make sure work progress.
QCE: monitor daily activities and verity work is in compliance with RC standards.
Safety Officer: Responsible for all safety aspects in the site.

Work Force: lists the (available/required) engineers, officers, labors, driver, nurse and electrician required for the job.

Health & Safety:

Contractual Requirements:
Maintain safety at all times, PPE, Monitor the safety of manpower, Stop the work when unsafe, Report any near miss or accident at work, Safety Officer available all the time during working hours.
Protective and Safety Equipment:
Proper PPE to do the job.
Emergency Procedures:
Any accident or incident happened will be immediately reported to RC, One male nurse with ambulance assigned to cover the night shift, First aid indecent treated in the first aid room.

Environmental Impact:

All excavated materials on the area was placed three meter away, Contractor to ensure materials from procured for the project are supplied with MSDS, Ensure correct storage of material, Risk assessment to be completed before any chemicals are brought to site, Respirators and dust masks are selected according to the hazard present.
