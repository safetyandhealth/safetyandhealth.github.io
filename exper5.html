Environment, Safety & Health Training Rotation at Bechtel SABCO


The safety lessons learnt and safety procedures performed during this site visit are the following:

1. Discuss scaffolding construction which involves competent person to erect the scaffolds, and the components which include: the based wood or metal to ensure that scaffold is at right angle. The base plate, bearers, runners, sills, swivel clamps, rigid clamp, joint connections. Moreover, discuss the color code associated with scaffold, Red: Do not use unsafe, Yellow: need inspection use with safety harness, Green: Safe to use.
2. Discuss and observe issue with Road Management: Roads must be maintained in good condition during the whole construction time, must be barricaded, must be equipped with warning signs, speed signs and detour sign.

3. Discuss and check the Generator safety: the generator shall operate by a competent electrician , the information of the competent must be provided, no spill of oil or fuel exist around the generator area, PPE and hearing protection must be worn around the generator, generator must be housekeep with door always closed.
4. Discuss and observe safety issue in laydown area: Laydown area is an area for load and unload of goods, equipment, and material. Material must be stored in a manner to avoid material damage, for example avoid erosion. Labors must wear suitable PPE all the time during their existing in the construction site.
5. Discuss and observe the concrete wash pit: it is used to avoid any environmental issue and it shows an important step toward the environment responsibilities.
