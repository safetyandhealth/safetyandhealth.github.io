STD-215 Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)


Minimum PPE

Helmet
Footwear
Eye
Vest
Gloves

General Guidance

PPE selected and provided by contractor to protect and provide(general safety, mechanical injury, inhalation and respiratory tract, skin, chemical, environment and all conform ANSI), employees shall be provided with necessary PPE, Contractor provide PPE info instructions, employees responsible for proper use of PPE provided, contractor replace defective PPE, supervisors ensure employees are trained, no wearing PPE not allowed to work, facilities to store all PPE provided by Contractor, PPE inspected before by user and RPE by supervisors and recorded

Clothing

  • Employees wear clothing that protect body and extremities complying with standard 205, loose clothing not wear near moving part, rings or necklaces not worn, body protection (extreme temp. sandblasting, welding, handling acids, clearing hazardous waste)

Head Protection

Helmets, shall be worn all times when (welders, construction plants, surveyors, worn in accordance with manufacturer require, painting of head protection prohibited, ES&H stuff responsible for red-colored head, supervisor wear yellow head protection for easy identification)

Eye and Face Protection

(Safety glasses/goggles, full face masks, sunglasses, welding goggles/masks), during day time glasses shall be tinted dark with UV protection, employee whose vision required corrective lenses wear personal eye glasses safety goggles, safety over-glasses.) Goggles worn (equipment grinders, drilling, lathes, chipping, mixing, all personal materials) welding operation/cutting/brazing/soldering mask, PPE as welder.

Safety Footwear

Steel Toe-cap insert, accordance to ANSI Z41, worn all time in construction site, Electrician must be supplied with footwear for their trade.

Respiratory Protection

RPE made available wording (containers, vessels, shafts, sewers, tanks, refrigerator, sand-blasting, spray-painting, diving) used according to manufacture instruction, according to MSDS.

Specialized Protection

Appropriate Work Plans, competent and trained do activities

Working at Height

Harnesses and associated equipment

Hearing Protection

Suitable hearing Protection for 85dB or above, mandatory to wear, warning sign shall be available, Hand and Arm Protection: Glove, (impacts, cuts, infections, temp., vibration, toxic, chemical)
