STD-206 Working at Height


Procedure

Contractor shall ensure that work at height is

Planned, supervised, safe, planning include risk assessments and safe sequence of works
proving equipment for control measures for working at height
work at height collective protection measures shall be first instance over personal measure
Take into account the working conditions/access and egress to the works to be conducted at height/distance and consequences of a fall, carried out at height, every contractor take sufficient measures to prevent any person/equipment or falling a distance liable to cause injury.
competent only work and supervised by a competent person
records shall be kept, ensure that work is not carried out at height when it can be done safely on ground
ensure that all work at height is carried out only when conditions do not jeopardize the health or safety of persons

Contractors shall ensure that in the case of (Scaffolding, working platform, man-lifts, fall arrest, ladders/steps/lightweight staging, roofing/man-baskets) comply with their standards
take suitable and sufficient steps to prevent the fall of any material or objects
no material or object is thrown or tipped from height where it is liable to cause personal injury or damage
ensure no risk of any person being struck by falling object
vehicle and mobile equipment either to fix or loosen loads
every person on or visiting the site shall report any activity or equipment defect relating to work at height
use work equipment and safety device provided to him for work at height with manufacturer instructions
pre-task briefing is conducted for all workers involved in the activity

Working at Height Procedures

  • 1- Avoid working at height
  • 2- Prevent falls - existing workplace.
  • 3- Prevent falls using collective equipment.
  • 4- Prevent falls using PPE in a way that prevents.
  • 5- Min distance using collective equipment.
  • 6- Min distance using PPE.
  • 7- Min consequences using collective equip.
  • 8- Min consequences through Training & Instruction.
