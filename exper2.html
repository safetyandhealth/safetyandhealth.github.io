Environment, Safety & Health Training Rotation at Bechtel SABCO


The safety lessons learnt and safety procedures performed during this site visit are the following:

1- Observe the site preparation and dump trucks and discuss safety requirements.
2- Observe and aware safety officer regarding cover for rotation parted.
3- The dump truck must be equipped with mesh net or trappers while travelling.
4- Aware safety officer about sharp edges and nails that should be covered, protected and removed.
5- Discuss heat index and that is must be communicate with employee at the construction site.
6- Discuss with the safety officer that employees must be competent and trained in order to do the assigned tasks.

7- Learn the safety with ladder and three points of contact.
8- Discuss the safety during installing glass windows and the vacuum machines required to lift glass windows.
9- Discuss the requirement of instructing employees of wear the appropriate PPE during performing the job and at the construction site.

10- Attend a coordination meeting between different contractors in order to establish communication and agreement regarding:
a- The housekeeping of the areas and the boundary of the areas of different contractors.
b- The safety issues inside the constructions such as: required PPE, the safety of lifting equipment for example cranes to have safety calculation.
c- Submit a waste plan on some basis such as weekly or every two week.
d- Inspection of the construction site.
