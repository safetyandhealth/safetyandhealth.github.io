Standards

Contractor shall provide the following welfare requirements both on the lay-down area and construction site, welfare areas are to be kept clear of construction traffic at all times, contractor shall provide awareness training on hygiene principles and use of facilities through induction training, labor camps are not permitted unless prior written approval from RC, laydown area plan must be approved by RC.

Rest Areas

Contractor shall provide designated rest area sufficient to hold the number of employees working in that area, no construction material/chemical/flammables are to be stored within the rest area, seating and tables are to be provide where reasonably practicable, rest areas are to be designated as no smoking.

Drinking Water

Only potable water shall be used for drinking, contractor shall supply cooled water at all times during the day for entire workforce, only approved potable water systems shall be used for source, contractor shall conduct a water quality test on a monthly basis, thermos drinking water coolers are to be cleared and disinfected every 2 days.

Food

Food on site is strictly prohibited without written RC, if permitted adequate facility for eating should be provided, there shall be no food preparation or cooking on site. Contractor shall make adequate arrangements for prayer.
In semi-permanent, contractor shall provide adequate storage and changing facilities in accordance with standard 207.
